=========
Icon Role
=========

The ``icon`` role allows you to render icons from the
`FontAwesome <https://fontawesome.com/>`__ icon font. This role also
generates a caption for screen readers, no-CSS browsers, and non-HTML
Sphinx builders.

This caption is automatically derived from the FontAwesome 5 class name
if not explicitly provided.

In your ``conf.py``, add ``'icon`` to the ``extensions``
list.

.. example::

   .. code-block:: rst

      Press the :icon:`windows` key. This has the caption ``windows icon``.

      Press the :icon:`Windows logo <windows>` key. This has the caption
      ``Windows logo``.

   Press the :icon:`windows` key. This has the caption ``windows icon``.

   Press the :icon:`Windows logo <windows>` key. This has the caption
   ``Windows logo``.

The following icons are supported:

.. list-table::

    * - ``caret-right``
      - :icon:`caret-right`

    * - ``caret-square-left``
      - :icon:`caret-square-left`

    * - ``caret-square-right``
      - :icon:`caret-square-right`

    * - ``check-circle``
      - :icon:`check-circle`

    * - ``check-square``
      - :icon:`check-square`

    * - ``edit``
      - :icon:`edit`

    * - ``exclamation-circle``
      - :icon:`exclamation-circle`

    * - ``pencil``
      - :icon:`pencil`

    * - ``save``
      - :icon:`save`

    * - ``trash``
      - :icon:`trash`

    * - ``trash-alt``
      - :icon:`trash-alt`
